Who Am I?

Hey, how's it going? I'm Alex, but for some reason I messed up a vowel and go by Alax on the web. I'm a wanna-be game design theorist. I use my background in critical analysis, philosophy and computer technology design to make high quality videos about video games. My series KNOW YOUR MOVES serves as a entry into the wide reaching world of Nintendo. With Super Smash Brothers, it allows me to analyse character's history, background and conception to truly understand how they came to be designed. Frankly, I just love these characters. Knowing I can educate a small nook of the web on the design of characters I love gives me those oh so wonderful warm fuzzies.
